Events

For exclusive events, Bridge Lane can tailor a bespoke package to match your vision. We cater to both corporate functions and private events and have a dedicated manager to ensure the seamless running of your event.

We utilise a nearby industry-leading caterer to deliver premium menus and will coordinate chefs and wait-staff either off-site or from our bar kitchen.

Our space provides our clients with the possibility of unique guest experiences and can be utilised in a number of different ways.

Christopher Hanna

Bridge Lane is brought to you by the team behind the Christopher Hanna Salon group.

Established over 10 years ago, Christopher Hanna is a luxury hair institution, with previous residencies in Surry Hills and the Ivy complex.

After securing a rare site in the historic Moran House on Bridge Street, we knew it was time for something exceptional - part salon, part cocktail bar, part beauty clinic, Christopher Hanna is a world of indulgence.
